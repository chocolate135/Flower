How flowers got their name

FLOWER NAMEFLOWER IMAGEDESCRIPTION
RoseFrom Latin rosa which has a uncertain origin but is probably from Greek rhodon
VioletFrom the latin word Viola from the same definition the color word developed from the flower name
MarigoldA fifteenth century word simply combining mary(in refernce to the virgan) and the color gold
Tulip Traces to the Turkish word tulbent meaning "turban", because the flower was thought to resemble one

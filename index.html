Introduction

A flower is the part of a plant that blossoms. Flowers produce the seeds that can become new plants. Most plants, including many trees, grow some kind of flower.

Physical Features

A flower usually has four main parts.

  1. Calyx
  2. Corolla
  3. Stamens
  4. Pistil

Pollination

Pollination is the process by which flowers form seeds. This is how plants reproduce.
There are two main kinds of pollination

  • Self polliation
  • Cross Pollination

Agent of pollination

There are various agents who help in pollination
  • Wind
  • Water
  • Birds
  • Bees
  • Other insects and animals
